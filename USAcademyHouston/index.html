    Questions & Answers

    Answers for busy parents

    The things families ask us most. Still have a question? We're always happy to talk.

    What ages does US Academy care for?

    We welcome children from 6 weeks old through age 12 — infants, toddlers, two-year-olds, preschool and pre-K, plus after-school care for school-age children. Each age group has its own room, teachers and age-appropriate program.

    What are your hours?

    US Academy is open Monday through Friday, 6:30 AM to 6:30 PM, to fit the schedules of working Houston families. Hourly and daily drop-in care is available by arrangement.

    Is US Academy a licensed childcare center?

    Yes. We operate as a licensed childcare center and follow Texas minimum standards for health, safety and staff-to-child ratios. We are happy to answer any licensing questions during your tour.

    Do you provide meals, and how do you handle allergies?

    We serve fresh, nutritionally balanced meals and snacks prepared on-site, with plenty of fruits, vegetables and whole grains — and our menus are pork-free. Just let us know about any allergies or dietary needs and we will plan around them carefully.

    How do I enroll my child?

    Start by sending an enrollment request through our website or giving us a call. We will arrange a tour, help you choose the right program, review availability, and walk you through the simple paperwork to get started.

    Do you offer part-time or drop-in care?

    Yes — alongside full-time care we offer flexible hourly and daily drop-in options, subject to availability. It is a great fit for appointments, part-time schedules or an occasional helping hand.

    What safety and security measures are in place?

    Our building has secure keypad entry, CCTV monitoring in classrooms and common areas, and a sign-in policy with photo ID for anyone picking up a child. Our staff are background-checked and trained in CPR and first aid.

    Can we tour the center before enrolling?

    Absolutely — and we encourage it. Schedule a visit and we will show you the classrooms, introduce you to our teachers, and answer every question so you feel completely confident.
