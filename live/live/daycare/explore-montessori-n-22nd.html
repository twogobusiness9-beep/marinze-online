Explore Montessori at 1933 N 22nd

Our north Bozeman campus on N 22nd Avenue — the same authentic Montessori philosophy, close to home for north-side families.

Montessori preschool &amp; infant daycare in north Bozeman

Our N 22nd campus serves infants through age 6 with Explore Piccolo, Pre-Primary, and Primary programs. Mixed-age classrooms, trained guides, outdoor learning, and daily Brightwheel communication give children a joyful, purposeful day.
