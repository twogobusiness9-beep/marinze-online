About Us

Founder &amp; Director

Meet Jessica Dehn

Jessica Dehn is a passionate educator, entrepreneur, and mother of three who has spent over a decade creating nurturing early-childhood environments. She founded Explore Montessori & Academy to offer families a nurturing, authentic Montessori experience in Bozeman.

In addition to Explore, Jessica is the founder of several early-childhood education ventures, including Dino Drop-In, Dino on the GO!, ABCDino Academy, and the Dino Discovery Experience — each designed to support modern families and spark a love of learning.

Jessica is deeply engaged in supporting families and women in business. She serves on the Bozeman Area Chamber of Commerce Board and has contributed to early-childhood initiatives across Montana.

“Children thrive when adults trust their potential, prepare the environment, and provide gentle guidance.”
