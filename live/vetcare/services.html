Our Services

Emergency, critical care &amp; specialty services

24-hour emergency care plus advanced referral specialties — all in one Toronto hospital, working alongside your primary veterinarian.

Emergency &amp; ICU

Open 24 hours a day, every day

You never need a referral for an emergency. Our emergency and intensive-care team is here around the clock to stabilize, diagnose, and treat critically ill and injured pets — with continuous monitoring and communication throughout.
