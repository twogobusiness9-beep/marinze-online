About VEC

Comfort, care &amp; compassion since 1974

For nearly 50 years, the Veterinary Emergency Clinic has been Toronto's trusted destination for after-hours and critical veterinary care.

Our Mission

An extension of your veterinarian's care

To provide excellence in patient care as an extension of the region's primary veterinary care practices through emergency, critical care and specialty services.

We are not a general practice — we don't offer routine services like vaccinations or spays and neuters. Instead, we're here for the moments in between: emergencies, critical illness, and the advanced specialty care that complements your regular vet.

What Guides Us

Our core values

💬

Communication

We keep pet owners and referring veterinarians informed at every step — you can call for an update on your pet day or night.

🩺

Triage

Every patient is assessed by severity so the most critical cases are seen first, and no emergency waits when it matters most.

🤝

Collaboration

We work in conjunction with your primary care veterinarian and promptly share medical records so follow-up care is seamless.

🕐

Accessibility

Open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, with two Toronto locations and on-site parking for when you need us.

Trusted &amp; Accredited

Recognized care you can rely on

VEC is accredited by the College of Veterinarians of Ontario and has been featured on NBC's "Vets Saving Pets." Nearly five decades of experience stand behind every case we see.
