About CCDC

A caring dental home, built around your child

Two board-certified pediatric dentists and a gentle, kid-focused team in Parker, Colorado.

Our Mission

Individualized care for YOUR child

We believe the best dental care is individualized to meet the specific needs of your child. Parents are welcome throughout every visit, procedures are explained calmly and in detail, and our whole approach is gentle, positive, and child-friendly — the foundation of a lifelong healthy smile.

Our Doctors

Board-certified pediatric dentists

Dr. C. Marc Jones

Board-Certified Pediatric Dentist

Dr. Jones earned his Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (2004) and his Doctor of Dental Medicine, magna cum laude, from the Boston University Goldman School of Dental Medicine (2010). He completed a two-year pediatric dental residency at Louisiana State University in affiliation with Children's Hospital of New Orleans, gaining extensive clinical, emergency, and hospital training in the growth and development of children.

He is a member of the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry, the American Dental Association, the Colorado Academy of Pediatric Dentistry, and the Metro Denver Dental Society. Raised in Appalachian North Carolina, Dr. Jones is married with two sons and actively coaches competitive baseball; he also enjoys live music, cooking, grilling, and travel.

Dr. Sati Khalsa

Board-Certified Pediatric Dentist

Dr. Khalsa earned a Bachelor of Science in Psychobiology from UCLA (2002) and a Doctor of Dental Surgery from the New York University College of Dentistry (2006). After a general practice residency at Wyckoff Heights Hospital in Brooklyn, she completed a pediatric dentistry internship and a two-year residency at the University at Buffalo, and earned American Board of Pediatric Dentistry certification in 2018.

She holds an adjunct faculty position with the Children's Hospital Colorado pediatric dentistry residency program and belongs to the Colorado Dental Association, the Metro Denver Dental Society, and the Colorado Academy of Pediatric Dentistry. She practiced general and public health dentistry in New York for nearly a decade and completed dental mission work in Senegal before relocating to Colorado in 2017. She enjoys art, crafts, musical theater, and travel.

Our Team
