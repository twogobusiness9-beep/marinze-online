Welcome to your child's dental home

The First Visit

What to expect

A friendly, caring introduction to our dental home — where lifelong relationships begin.

🤝

A warm welcome

We introduce your child to the office at their pace. Parents are welcome to accompany their child throughout the entire visit.

🔎

A comprehensive exam

A gentle, thorough dental exam plus homecare instruction, nutrition counseling, growth and development evaluation, and orthodontic screening.

👶

Start by age one

We recommend a first visit by age one, following the American Academy of Pediatrics and the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry.

📋

What to bring

Bring your dental insurance information if you have it, and complete the new patient forms beforehand or when you arrive.

Patient Forms

Dental Emergencies

Tooth pain or injury? Call us right away

For a toothache, a bump to the mouth, or a knocked-out tooth, call our office as soon as possible so we can guide you and arrange prompt care for your child.
