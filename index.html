Built to Be Found

SEO & GEO — the difference is night and day.

A beautiful website that no one can find is a missed opportunity. I build sites to be discovered by both search engines and the new wave of AI-powered search.

SEO — Search Engine Optimization

Helping your business rank higher on Google and attract customers through organic search.

Weak SEO

  • Messy heading structure
  • Missing keywords & metadata
  • Thin, unstructured content
  • Poor internal linking

Strong SEO

  • Clear heading hierarchy
  • Optimized titles & metadata
  • Structured, keyword-rich content
  • Smart internal linking

Result: more visibility, more traffic, more leads.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimization

Preparing your website for AI-driven search — where customers increasingly ask, not just Google.

Weak GEO

  • Unclear, disorganized content
  • No schema / structured data
  • Poor semantic structure
  • Hard for AI to understand

Strong GEO

  • Clear, structured answers
  • Schema markup & clean data
  • Question & answer content
  • AI-friendly, easy to cite

Result: visible in ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews & Perplexity.

What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and why does it matter?

GEO prepares your website to be understood and cited by AI-powered answer engines — ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity — not just traditional search. It relies on clear structure, schema markup and question-and-answer content, on top of standard SEO.
